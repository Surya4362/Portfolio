Automated LinkedIn Outreach System for B2B Leads

Multi-step connection and message sequence using Waalaxy + Konnector.ai

3x

Challenge

  • Manual LinkedIn outreach was unsustainable and inconsistent
  • No structured follow-up system — replies got lost or delayed
  • Needed a scalable process that didn't feel spammy

System Built

  • Defined ICP: role, industry, company size filters in Sales Navigator
  • Waalaxy campaign: Connect → Delay 24h → Message 1 → Delay 48h → Message 2
  • Konnector.ai used to enrich profile data and track response status
  • Replies routed to a Google Sheet for daily follow-up tracking
  • Message copy tested across 2 variants — value-first vs. curiosity hook

Result

  • Consistently reached 3× the contacts vs. manual effort
  • ~40% average LinkedIn connection acceptance rate
  • Eliminated manual DM management entirely — zero daily effort on sending

Automation Deep Dive

How I build
lead systems, not just flows.

Here's an example of a real webhook-based automation I built — showing the thinking behind it, not just the tools.

Live Workflow · Lead Capture Pipeline
1

Webhook Trigger

A lead fills a form (Meta Instant Form, Typeform, or landing page). The data hits a webhook URL, firing the automation instantly.

POST /webhook → n8n / Make
2

Data Capture & Parsing

Name, email, phone, and source are extracted. Formatting is standardized (lowercase email, trimmed names) before anything else happens.

JSON parse → field mapping
3

Router / Conditional Logic

A router checks the lead source or intent signal and branches accordingly — hot leads go to one path, cold to another.

IF source = "meta" → Path A
4

Timed Delays & Sequencing

Each path runs on delays — 10 min, 24h, 48h. This prevents leads from being bombarded and keeps the sequence feeling human.

Wait node → 24h → next step
5

Storage + Notification

Lead is written to Google Sheets (for tracking), added to Brevo (for email), and a Slack/email alert goes to the sales team.

Sheets + Brevo + Slack alert

⚡ Why Webhooks?

Webhooks are real-time — the moment a lead submits, the workflow fires. No polling, no delays, no missed leads sitting in a queue.

🔀 Why Branching Logic?

Not every lead deserves the same path. A hot inbound lead and a cold LinkedIn connection need different nurture sequences — branching makes that automatic.

⏱️ Why Delays?

Immediate follow-up on everything feels robotic. Strategic delays — modeled after human response patterns — improve open rates and reply rates significantly.

📈 Why This Scales

Once built, the system handles 10 leads or 1,000 identically. There's no manual step in the loop — the team just sees qualified leads arrive, ready to close.
